LD 29 (Theme:Beneath the surface) - Playbuck

Controls:

  • Left-right: Movement
  • Space: Jump
  • D: Dig on light tiles
  • F: If next to a doe, "Le sexy time"
  • M: Music on/off
  • R: Restart

Objective:

You're the only male rabbit in the burrow. Try to ensure the survival of the group by procreating with all the does scattered around the level while you try to avoid (or jump over) the snakes.
