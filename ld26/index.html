LD 26 (Theme:Minimalism) - Sociales Santillana

How to play:

You have to match as many groups of 3 or more blocks of the same colour horizontally or vertically. To do that, click on a block to select it and then click on an adjacent one to swap it vertically or rotate the whole ring left or right.

Additional controls:

  • S: Shuffle the colours (It'll cost you 5s unless you're playing in timeless mode or if you're on the game over countdown)
  • H: It gives you a hint, selecting for you a block with a valid action, for 3s
  • I: Toggles infinite mode. No time restrictions, but no level change either
  • Backspace: If you've died, restart the game
